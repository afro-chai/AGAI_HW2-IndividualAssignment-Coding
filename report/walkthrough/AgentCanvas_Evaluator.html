Role

Synthesizes News, Volatility, and Moral angles after asyncio.gather completes for all three strategies on the same ticker.

Branching logic (evaluator.py)

  • Full agreement — all three decisions equal → task stresses different emphases despite the same label.
  • Graded pair agrees, Moral may differ — explain A/B convergence plus Moral’s angle.
  • Graded disagreement — explain mechanism (for example empty news vs tail-risk gates).
  • pattern_note is always derived from (News, Vol, Moral) decisions in Python.
