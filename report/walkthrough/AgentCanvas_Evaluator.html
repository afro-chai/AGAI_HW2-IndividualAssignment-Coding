Role

Consumes three fixed structured outputs. Must explain why philosophies converge or diverge—not only restate BUY/HOLD/SELL labels.

Branching logic (evaluator.py)

  • Full agreement — all three decisions equal → task stresses different emphases despite same label.
  • Graded pair agrees, Moral may differ — explain A/B convergence + Moral’s angle.
  • Graded disagreement — explain mechanism (e.g. empty news vs tail-risk gates).
  • pattern_note is always derived from (News, Vol, Moral) decisions in Python.
